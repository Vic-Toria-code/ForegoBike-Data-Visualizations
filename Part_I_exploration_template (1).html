by (Victoria Nyaguthii)¶

Introduction¶

This Project explores a dataset containing features of a bike hiring service including the information on durations made and a bit of info on the bike users.

The exploration includes three stages

a)Univariate Exploration¶

-What is the distribution of bike_share_for_all_trip -Whats the member_gender usage percentage -What are the types and distributuin of user_type -What are the differtent distributions of some of the numerical features -Which stations did the most trips start from?

b)Bivariate Exploration¶

-What is the bike_share_for_all_trip for all the member_gender -What is the bike_share_for_all_trip for all the user_type -How is the user distribution on member_gender and user_type -Whats the correltaion between duration_sec for each member_gender -What is the bike_share_for_all_trip for the duration_sec -Whats the relation of bike_id with the duration_sec

c)Multivariate Exploration¶

-what is the correltaion of all the features in the dataset -What is the correlation between duration_sec,member_gender and member_birth_year -What is the correlation between member_gender,duration_sec and user_type
